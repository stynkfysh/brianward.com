Bonds (Bail) Appraisal Service

Bail situations require fast, reliable property valuations. Brian Ward Appraisal provides expedited appraisals that document fair market value and available equity, meeting the standards expected by bail bondsmen and the courts.

Quick Turnaround

Same-day or next-day availability for expedited situations when time is critical.

Desktop Options

Desktop appraisals are often acceptable for bond purposes, delivering results without a property visit.

Bondsman Coordination

Bail industry standards are well understood, and coordination occurs directly with bondsmen on format and requirements.

Collateral Assessment

Documents fair market value and available equity for accurate loan-to-value ratio calculations.
