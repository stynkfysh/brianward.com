May 1, 2026

San Diego, 92105 — Market Conditions Update

The San Diego 92105 market is a seller's market as of the May 1, 2026 effective date. The median sale price for the most recent quarter is $920,000, up modestly from $912,000 in the prior quarter, while the median price per square foot rose to $800.59, a +7.1% quarterly gain. Inventory is tight at 2.1 months, supporting an anticipated marketing time of 30–60 days. Median days on market shortened to 16 from 24, and the sale-to-list ratio firmed to 100.0%. Quarterly sales volume rose +7.1% (181 closings versus 169), with year-over-year activity broadly stable; the size-normalized $/SF gain is the more reliable read on market direction.
