Pre-Purchase Home Appraisal Service

A pre-purchase appraisal gives you an independent, professional opinion of a property's value before you commit to buying. Unlike a real estate agent's CMA, an appraisal is a thorough, unbiased analysis you can use with confidence in negotiations.

Independent Valuation

A thorough, objective professional valuation — more rigorous and reliable than a real estate agent's CMA.

Negotiation Leverage

If the property is overpriced, our appraisal gives you documented evidence to negotiate a better deal.

Buyer Protection

Identifies potential issues and market factors that could affect the property's value now and in the future.

Quick Turnaround

Fast appraisals to keep pace with your purchasing timeline. Desktop options available for even faster results.

Real Savings for Real Buyers

A recent client in Pacific Beach ordered a pre-purchase appraisal before making an offer. Our appraisal showed the property was worth significantly less than the asking price, enabling the buyer to negotiate the price down by $40,000 — far more than the cost of the appraisal itself.
