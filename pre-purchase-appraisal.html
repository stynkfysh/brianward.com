Pre-Purchase Home Appraisal Service

Thank you for considering our Pre-Purchase Home Appraisal Service.

Buying a home is one of the largest investments you will make. Before submitting an offer, an independent professional appraisal can provide you with an unbiased opinion of fair market value. This knowledge empowers you to make informed negotiation decisions and avoid overpaying for your home.

Our pre-purchase appraisals are characterized by:

  • Independent Value Opinion: Unlike a real estate agent's Comparative Market Analysis (CMA), our appraisal is a thorough, objective professional valuation based on detailed market research and property analysis.
  • Negotiation Leverage: An appraisal showing the property is overpriced gives you concrete data to support a lower offer. Many buyers have saved thousands by using appraisals to negotiate better prices.
  • Informed Decision Making: You receive a detailed report explaining how we arrived at the value, giving you confidence in your purchasing decision.
  • Buyer Protection: A professional appraisal identifies potential issues and market factors that could affect the property's true value.
  • Quick Turnaround: We provide fast appraisals to support your purchasing timeline, whether you need a standard inspection-based appraisal or a quicker desktop option.

One of our Pacific Beach clients received an appraisal showing the property was worth significantly less than the asking price, enabling them to negotiate the sale price down by up to $40,000. A pre-purchase appraisal is one of the best investments you can make before committing to a major purchase.
