Welcome to our Market Conditions blog where we share insights from our real estate market analyses across San Diego County and surrounding areas. April 9, 2026

Fallbrook, 92028 — Vacant Land Market Conditions Update

The Fallbrook 92028 area is a balanced market as of the April 9, 2026 effective date. Because land sales in the immediate area are too sparse for statistically meaningful trends, residential market data is presented as the best available indicator of overall conditions. The median sale price for the most recent quarter is $867,500, down from $961,000 in the prior quarter (-9.7%), while the median price per square foot rose to $437.14, a +4.0% quarterly gain. Inventory stands at 4.5 months, consistent with an anticipated marketing time of 60–90 days. Median days on market edged up to 40 from 37, the sale-to-list ratio held at 100.0%, and quarterly sales volume fell -25.5% (108 closings versus 145), pointing to a moderating market. (Effective date approximated from the report file date.)

April 9, 2026

Santee, 92071 — Vacant Land Market Conditions Update

The Santee 92071 vacant-land market is a buyer's market that is softening as of the April 9, 2026 effective date (taken from the report's file date, as the source report did not state one). Because vacant-land transactions in the immediate area are too thin to model directly, the broader Santee residential market is presented as the best available proxy: the median sale price for the most recent quarter is $867,500, down from $961,000 in the prior quarter (-9.7%), while the median price per square foot firmed to $437.14 (+4.0%), with inventory at 4.5 months and an anticipated marketing time of 60-90 days. On the land side, the most recent quarter recorded 19 closed sales at a median of $290,000 (median 3.04 acres, $1.27 per lot SF), a 94.0% sale-to-list ratio, 45 median days on market, and roughly 8.7 months of land supply. Together the metrics point to a thinly traded land market where buyers are negotiating more aggressively than in the residential segment.

April 6, 2026

Encinitas, 92024 — Market Conditions Update

The Encinitas 92024 market is a seller's market as of the April 6, 2026 effective date. The median sale price for the most recent quarter is $2,386,378, up from $2,115,500 in the prior quarter, while the median price per square foot eased to $952.34, a -6.4% quarterly move. Inventory is tight at 2.6 months, supporting an anticipated marketing time of 30–60 days. Median days on market shortened sharply to 14 from 35, and the sale-to-list ratio strengthened to 100.0%. Quarterly sales volume rose +11.1% (80 closings versus 72), with year-over-year activity expanding, reflecting active demand in this coastal market.

Palm Desert, 92260 — Market Conditions Update

The Palm Desert 92260 condominium/attached segment is a buyer's market as of the April 3, 2026 effective date, reflecting substantial oversupply. The median sale price for the most recent quarter is $489,000, down from $530,000 in the prior quarter, while the median price per square foot eased to $328.33, a -2.3% quarterly move.
