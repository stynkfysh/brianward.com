Pre-Sale Home Appraisal Service

Thank you for considering our Pre-Sale Home Appraisal Service.

Setting the right asking price is critical to a successful home sale. Price too high and your home sits on the market, attracting fewer qualified buyers. Price too low and you leave money on the table. A professional pre-listing appraisal provides the objective data needed to establish an accurate asking price that attracts buyers and maximizes your sale proceeds.

Our pre-sale appraisals are characterized by:

  • Accurate Pricing Strategy: Our detailed appraisal provides solid foundation for listing your home at its true market value, attracting serious buyers and supporting a faster sale.
  • More Detailed Than a CMA: While real estate agents provide Comparative Market Analyses (CMAs), our professional appraisals go deeper with comprehensive property analysis, market research, and defensible valuation conclusions.
  • Avoid Appraisal Surprises: When a buyer obtains their own appraisal during escrow, an inflated asking price can jeopardize the sale or lead to renegotiation. Our appraisal helps prevent this problem before listing.
  • Pricing Confidence: You receive a detailed report explaining the market factors, comparable sales, and property condition analysis that support the valuation. This confidence helps you respond to offers strategically.
  • Market Support: In changing markets, our appraisal documents current conditions and recent comparable sales, providing support for your pricing decisions.

A pre-listing appraisal is an investment that pays for itself by helping you price your home correctly from the start, attracting qualified buyers and supporting a smoother, faster sale at the best possible price.
