Estate Appraisal Service

Estate appraisals provide the foundation for equitable distribution of assets, proper tax filings, and legal compliance during probate and trust administration. Flexible report types are available — standard appraisal, desktop, or specialized estate report — tailored to your specific needs.

Accurate Valuations

Fair market value assessments ensuring equitable distribution and full legal compliance.

Legal Professional Experience

Regular work with attorneys, executors, and trustees who rely on the reports for estate administration.

USPAP Compliance

Every report complies with the Uniform Standards of Professional Appraisal Practice.

Clear Documentation

Thorough reports establishing property value at time of death or other relevant dates for all parties.

Related Services

Additional services include Date of Death Appraisal, Proposition 19 Transfer Appraisal, Tax Appraisal, Family Transaction Appraisal, and Appraisal Review. View all services on the Services & Fees page.
