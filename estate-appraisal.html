Estate Appraisal Service

Thank you for considering our Estate Appraisal Service.

Estate appraisals are essential when managing the affairs of a deceased person's property. Whether you are an executor, trustee, beneficiary, or attorney handling estate administration, an accurate professional valuation is crucial for equitable distribution of assets and compliance with tax and legal requirements.

Our estate appraisals are characterized by:

  • Accurate Valuations: We provide fair market value assessments that ensure equitable distribution among beneficiaries and comply with probate and trust administration requirements.
  • Experience with Legal Professionals: We work regularly with attorneys, executors, and trustees, understanding the specific documentation and reporting standards required for estate proceedings.
  • USPAP Compliance: Our reports are prepared in accordance with the Uniform Standards of Professional Appraisal Practice (USPAP) and are accepted by courts and legal professionals.
  • Clear Documentation: We provide thorough appraisal reports that clearly establish the property's value at the time of death or other relevant dates, supporting tax filings and legal proceedings.

Whether you need a standard appraisal, desktop valuation, or specialized estate report, we tailor our services to meet the specific requirements of your situation. Our goal is to provide you with a credible, defensible appraisal that facilitates the smooth administration of the estate.
