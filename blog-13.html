Market Conditions Reports

Welcome to our Market Conditions blog where we share insights from our real estate market analyses across San Diego County and surrounding areas. Each report examines median home prices, inventory levels, days on market, and price trends based on MLS data. Check back regularly for the latest updates.

January 23, 2026

La Mesa, 91941 — Market Conditions Update

The La Mesa 91941 market is a seller's market that is directionally weakening as of the January 23, 2026 effective date. The median sale price for the most recent quarter is $1,067,500, down from $1,135,000 in the prior quarter, while the median price per square foot held essentially flat at $550.36, a -0.5% quarterly move. Inventory is tight at 1.4 months, supporting an anticipated marketing time of 30–60 days. Median days on market shortened to 30 from 35, and the sale-to-list ratio strengthened to 100.0%, with well-priced listings selling at or above ask. Quarterly sales volume eased -7.7% (60 closings versus 65), though year-over-year activity expanded (125 sales over the trailing six months versus 75 a year earlier). A negative 12-month linear $/SF trend (-5.9%) against a firmer +1.5% six-month trend indicates values that softened over the year but have begun to stabilize recently.

December 18, 2025

Palm Desert, 92260 — Market Conditions Update

The Palm Desert 92260 market is a buyer's market as of the December 18, 2025 effective date, reflecting significant oversupply. The median sale price for the most recent quarter is $611,050, up from $579,000 in the prior quarter, and the median price per square foot rose to $327.85, a +2.2% quarterly gain. Inventory is high at 21.6 months, consistent with an anticipated marketing time of 180+ days. Median days on market lengthened to 82 from 76, and the sale-to-list ratio held at 98.0%. Quarterly sales volume rose +14.8% (217 closings versus 189), with year-over-year activity broadly stable, indicating steady demand against an ample supply backdrop.

November 2, 2025

Palm Springs, 92262 — Market Conditions Update

The Palm Springs 92262 market is in a declining phase as of the November 2, 2025 effective date, based on Riverside County public records closed-sales data. Over the trailing 12 months, the overall median sale price was $985,000; the most recent quarter posted a median of $921,250, down -5.2% from the prior quarter, with median price per square foot at $485.96, a -8.2% quarterly decline. The current six-month median sits -8.5% below the year-ago period, and fitted 12-month linear trends are negative on overall median price (-22.6%) and the size-normalized 2,000–3,200 SF bracket (-13.9%). Monthly closed-sale volume remained active at 31–52 transactions, indicating a market that is repricing downward rather than stalling. Supply-side metrics including inventory, days on market, and list-to-sale ratios are not available from this public-records data source and are therefore omitted.
