April 28, 2025

Palm Desert, 92260 — Market Conditions Update

The Palm Desert market (ZIP codes 92211 and 92260, all property types) is best characterized as broadly stable with modest, mix-weighted appreciation as of the retrospective April 28, 2025 effective date. The median sale price for the quarter ending on the effective date is $593,000, down from $617,500 in the prior quarter (-4.0%), while the median price per square foot firmed to $339.64, a +0.9% quarterly gain — a divergence that reflects sale-mix rotation rather than an underlying value change. Supply was tight at an estimated 1.5 months (a floor estimate drawn from the closed-sale dataset), supporting a seller-leaning balance and an anticipated marketing time of 30–60 days. Median marketing time for sold properties was approximately 52 days, and the sale-to-list ratio held steady at 97.7% versus 97.9% the prior quarter. Quarterly sales volume rose +48.0% (521 closings versus 352), consistent with the normal winter-to-spring seasonal pattern in this desert resort market. With a 12-month linear trend of +4.3% per year for median sale price and an essentially flat -0.6% per year for median $/SF, the size-normalized metric indicates a broadly stable market with modest headline appreciation concentrated in late 2024.

March 29, 2025

San Diego, 92129 — Market Conditions Update (2025)

The San Diego 92129 (Rancho Peñasquitos) market was a seller's market as of the March 29, 2025 effective date. The median sale price for the most recent quarter was $1,529,500, up from $1,430,000 in the prior quarter, while the median price per square foot rose to $741.56, a +3.5% quarterly gain. Inventory was tight at 2.5 months, supporting an anticipated marketing time of 30–60 days. Median days on market held steady at 17, and the sale-to-list ratio strengthened to 100.0%. Quarterly sales volume fell -34.5% (38 closings versus 58), though year-over-year activity was expanding.

December 5, 2024

Coronado, 92118 — Market Conditions Update

The Coronado 92118 market is a balanced market as of the December 5, 2024 effective date. The median sale price for the most recent quarter is $3,100,000, up from $2,992,000 in the prior quarter, while the median price per square foot eased to $1,490.99, a -1.6% quarterly move. Inventory stands at 4.1 months, consistent with an anticipated marketing time of 60–90 days. Median days on market shortened sharply to 44 from 66, and the sale-to-list ratio held at 96.0%, with buyers negotiating modest discounts from ask in this high-value coastal market. Quarterly sales volume declined -14.7% (29 closings versus 34), even as year-over-year activity expanded.
