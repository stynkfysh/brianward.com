Appraisal Services & Fees

Transparent pricing for appraisal services in San Diego County

90% or more of all residential properties fall under our standard pricing structure

We offer multiple appraisal service options designed to meet different needs and budgets. Choose the service that best fits your situation, whether you need a comprehensive appraisal or a faster, more economical option.

Our Services and Pricing

Understanding Your Service Options

Standard Appraisal

Starting at $450

The most comprehensive appraisal option. Includes full interior and exterior inspection, detailed property analysis, and thorough comparable sales research.

When to Use:

  • Mortgage lending
  • Refinancing
  • Home equity loans
  • Estate planning
  • Most legal proceedings

Consumer Appraisal

Starting at $475

Similar to Standard Appraisal with detailed analysis and inspection. Designed for various purposes including estate settlement, tax bases, and legal proceedings.

When to Use:

  • Estate settlement
  • Date of death valuations
  • Divorce proceedings
  • Bankruptcy cases
  • Tax purposes

Drive-By Appraisal

Starting at $350

Exterior inspection and photograph with comparable sales analysis. No interior inspection. Faster turnaround than full appraisals.

When to Use:

  • Refinancing scenarios
  • Property assessment
  • Quick value estimates
  • When interior access is unavailable
  • Speed is important

Desktop Appraisal

Starting at $300

Value estimate using publicly available data and property records. No property inspection. Most economical option, fastest turnaround.

When to Use:

  • Quick value checks
  • Vacant land
  • Initial assessment
  • Limited budgets
  • Non-critical valuations

2-4 Unit Property

Starting at $550

Specialized appraisal for duplex, triplex, or four-plex properties. Includes full inspection and analysis specific to multi-unit residential.

When to Use:

  • Multi-unit property purchase
  • Investment property lending
  • Refinancing multi-unit
  • Estate planning
  • Legal proceedings
