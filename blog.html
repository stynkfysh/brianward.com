Welcome to our Market Conditions blog where we share insights from our real estate market analyses across San Diego County and surrounding areas. Each report examines median home prices, inventory levels, days on market, and price trends based on MLS data. Check back regularly for the latest updates.

April 9, 2026

Fallbrook, 92028 — Vacant Land Market Conditions Update

This Fallbrook-area vacant land analysis uses residential market data as a proxy, supplemented by a dedicated land sales analysis. The residential market shows balanced conditions with 4.5 months of inventory and a median sale price of $867,500, down 9.7% from the prior quarter. Median price per square foot rose to $437.14, with a +7.84% twelve-month linear trend. Days on market edged up from 37 to 40, and the SP/LP ratio held at 100.0%. The anticipated marketing time of 60–90 days reflects moderate absorption in this area.
