San Diego County Housing Market Update - Q1 2024

Published: March 15, 2024

The San Diego housing market continues to show resilience with notable trends emerging in the first quarter of 2024. Our latest analysis reveals that median home prices in San Diego County have stabilized after market volatility in previous quarters. The average price per square foot has increased by 3.2% compared to the same period last year, while inventory levels remain relatively low at approximately 2.5 months supply. Days on market for residential properties average 28 days, indicating a competitive marketplace. Buyers continue to focus on properties with updated amenities and energy-efficient features, particularly in coastal communities and established neighborhoods. The rental market similarly shows strength with year-over-year rent appreciation of 4.1% across the county.

Understanding Date of Death Appraisals: What Estate Executors Need to Know

Published: March 8, 2024

When managing an estate, obtaining an accurate date of death appraisal is often a critical requirement. Also known as a "step-up in basis" appraisal, this valuation is essential for determining the fair market value of real property at the time of the deceased person's death. This value becomes the new basis for calculating capital gains taxes for beneficiaries who inherit the property. Understanding the importance and process of this appraisal can help executors fulfill their fiduciary duties effectively and help beneficiaries avoid potential tax complications. In this article, we explore the key elements of date of death appraisals, including why they're necessary, what appraisers examine, how market conditions affect valuations, and common mistakes to avoid. Whether you're administering an estate or inheriting property, this information will help you navigate the appraisal process with confidence.

Bankruptcy and Your Home: How a Real Estate Appraisal Helps

Published: February 28, 2024

Facing bankruptcy is undoubtedly stressful, and the role of real estate appraisals in this process is often misunderstood. An accurate property appraisal becomes essential in bankruptcy proceedings to establish the fair market value of your home, which directly impacts your equity position and repayment obligations. Whether you're considering Chapter 7 or Chapter 13 bankruptcy, creditors and bankruptcy trustees rely on professional appraisals to determine asset values. For homeowners looking to keep their residence, understanding how appraisals affect exemptions and equity positions is crucial. We explore how bankruptcy appraisals differ from standard appraisals, the typical timeline for obtaining them, and how accurate valuations can influence the outcome of your case. If you're navigating bankruptcy and own real estate, proper appraisal documentation can make a significant difference in protecting your financial interests and achieving the best possible resolution.
