Market Conditions Reports

Welcome to our Market Conditions blog where we share insights from our real estate market analyses across San Diego County and surrounding areas. Each report examines median home prices, inventory levels, days on market, and price trends based on MLS data. Check back regularly for the latest updates.

May 20, 2026

Bonita, 91902 — Market Conditions Update

The Bonita 91902 market is a seller's market that is directionally strengthening as of the May 20, 2026 effective date. The median sale price for the most recent quarter is $1,225,000, down from $1,300,000 in the prior quarter, while the median price per square foot is $531.31, a -3.7% quarterly move. Inventory is tight at 2.1 months, consistent with an anticipated marketing time of 30–60 days. Median days on market lengthened to 22 from 16, a sign of modest buyer caution even as the sale-to-list ratio firmed to 101.0%. Quarterly sales volume rose +28.6% (27 closings versus 21), and the size-normalized $/SF metric remains the more reliable read on underlying market direction.

May 20, 2026

San Diego, 92109 — Market Conditions Update

The San Diego 92109 (Pacific Beach) market is a strengthening seller's market as of the May 20, 2026 effective date. The median sale price for the most recent quarter is $2,375,000, up from $2,282,450 in the prior quarter, and the median price per square foot climbed to $1,235.01, an +11.8% quarterly gain. Inventory remains tight at 2.6 months, supporting an anticipated marketing time of 30–60 days. Median days on market held essentially steady at 28 versus 26, and the sale-to-list ratio is stable at 97.0%. Quarterly sales volume jumped +37.9% (40 closings versus 29), indicating active demand at the high end of this coastal market.

Valley Center, 92082 — Market Conditions Update
