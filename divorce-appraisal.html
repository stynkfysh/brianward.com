Professional appraisals specifically designed for family law proceedings in San Diego

Experience with Divorce Appraisals

Divorce appraisals are one of our specialties. We conduct more divorce appraisals than most appraisers in the San Diego area, which means we understand the unique requirements and sensitivities involved in marriage dissolution proceedings.

Real estate is often a significant asset in divorce settlements, and accurate, credible appraisals are essential for fair division of property. Our appraisals provide the documentation and professional presentation you need for successful legal proceedings.

Our Commitment to Quality

  • Diligent research with credible, trustworthy comparable properties
  • Thorough documentation and clear professional presentation
  • Sensitivity to the emotional and financial aspects of dissolution
  • Strict confidentiality regarding your personal information
  • Post-delivery support and consultation as needed
  • Expertise in court testimony when required

Expertise in Backdated and Retrospective Appraisals

Over 1000 backdated and retrospective appraisals have been completed by our team. In many divorce cases, property needs to be appraised as of a specific date in the past, such as the date of separation or the date the marriage began. We have the expertise and experience to handle these complex appraisals accurately.

Unlike standard mortgage appraisals, divorce appraisals are not bound by lender restrictions. This allows us to provide a complete and accurate analysis tailored specifically to your legal needs.

Differences from Mortgage Appraisals

Divorce Appraisals

  • No lender guidelines or restrictions
  • Can be backdated to any period
  • Tailored to legal requirements
  • May include expert testimony
  • Focuses on fair market value only

Mortgage Appraisals

  • Bound by lender requirements
  • Only current date appraisals
  • Focused on lending value
  • Not designed for legal use
  • Standard format and methodology

Frequently Asked Questions

What type of appraisal should I order for my divorce? +

We typically recommend a Consumer Appraisal or Standard Appraisal for divorce proceedings. These include full property inspection and detailed analysis suitable for legal proceedings. The choice depends on your specific situation and court requirements. Contact us to discuss which option is best for your case.

Can you appraise the property as of the date of separation? +

Yes, absolutely. Backdated appraisals are one of our specialties. We can appraise the property as of the date of separation, date of marriage, or any other date relevant to your divorce. We'll research historical market data and comparable sales from that period to provide an accurate appraisal.

How is confidentiality handled? +

We take confidentiality very seriously. Your personal and financial information is protected throughout the appraisal process. We understand the sensitive nature of divorce proceedings and maintain strict privacy standards. Only necessary information is included in the appraisal report.

Will I need to testify about the appraisal in court? +

Not necessarily. In many cases, an appraisal report is sufficient without testimony. However, if the other party challenges the appraisal or if expert testimony is needed, we are experienced in court appearances and expert witness testimony. We can discuss this with you when you order your appraisal.
