  • Ongoing Support: We provide consultation and support after report delivery, including potential court testimony if needed.

Divorce Appraisals vs. Mortgage Appraisals

Divorce appraisals differ significantly from mortgage appraisals. Mortgage lenders often put restrictions and requirements on the appraiser with their goal being first to ensure the sale of the loan they are funding in the secondary mortgage market. In contrast, divorce appraisals are tailored specifically to your legal needs and are not bound by lender guidelines or restrictions.

Expertise in Backdated and Retrospective Appraisals

We have completed over 1,000 backdated and retrospective appraisals, dating back up to 28 years in the past. In many divorce cases, property needs to be appraised as of a specific date in the past, such as the date of separation or the date the marriage began. We have the expertise and historical market knowledge to handle these complex assignments accurately using stringent appraisal standards.
