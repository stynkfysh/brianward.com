Real Estate Appraisal Services in San Diego and Riverside Counties, California

Brian Ward Appraisal provides comprehensive real estate appraisal services throughout San Diego and Riverside Counties. Whether you need an appraisal for bankruptcy, divorce, estate purposes, tax assessment, or any other specialized appraisal need, we serve all major communities across both counties.

With 22 years of experience and more than 7,000 completed appraisals, we understand the local real estate market and provide detailed, credible valuations that hold up to professional scrutiny.
