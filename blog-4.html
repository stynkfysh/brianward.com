Market Conditions Reports

June 26, 2025

Fallbrook, 92028 — Market Conditions Update

The Fallbrook residential market is balanced with 4.2 months of inventory and modest broad-based appreciation. The median sale price of $952,375 was essentially flat (−0.3%), while median price per square foot rose 5.4% to $442, with a 12-month linear trend of +5.0%. The SP/LP ratio reached 100%, confirming strong pricing discipline. Sales volume increased 17.2% to 143 transactions. Days on market rose from 31 to 50, reflecting seasonal inventory absorption. The anticipated marketing time of 60–90 days reflects orderly conditions with neither buyers nor sellers holding a decisive advantage.

February 25, 2025

La Mesa, 91941 — Market Conditions Update

The La Mesa market shows seller’s market conditions with 2.3 months of inventory and a 30–60 day marketing time. The median sale price of $890,000 declined 8.3% from the prior quarter, though median price per square foot held essentially flat at $585 (+0.3%), suggesting compositional shifts rather than broad value erosion. Days on market improved 38.7% to just 19 days, and the SP/LP ratio held at 100%. Year-over-year sales volume is up 16.7%, indicating sustained demand. This market is best characterized as undergoing price normalization within a tight-supply environment.

May 1, 2024

Rancho Mirage, 92270 — Market Conditions Update

The Rancho Mirage single-family market shows a stabilizing environment with 4.9 months of inventory — at the upper boundary of balanced conditions. The median sale price of $1,177,000 rose 1.9% quarter-over-quarter, with 12-month linear appreciation of +14.4% in both prices and price per square foot. However, the 6-month $/SF trend has turned negative at −2.9%, indicating moderation from the January 2024 peak. Days on market rose to 54 and the SP/LP ratio held at 97%. Sales volume surged 58.7% to 146 transactions, reflecting strong buyer activity in this desert resort community.
