Market Conditions Reports

Welcome to our Market Conditions blog where we share insights from our real estate market analyses across San Diego County and surrounding areas. Each report examines median home prices, inventory levels, days on market, and price trends based on MLS data. Check back regularly for the latest updates.

May 13, 2026

Escondido, 92026 — Market Conditions Update

The Escondido 92026 market is a seller’s market as of the May 13, 2026 effective date, though directional indicators point to modest softening. The median sale price for the most recent quarter is $920,000, up 1.7% from $905,000 in the prior quarter, while the median price per square foot held essentially flat at $485.47. Inventory is tight at 2.1 months of supply across 187 active listings, supporting an anticipated marketing time of 30–60 days. Median days on market edged up to 27 from 24, a hint of buyer caution, even as the sale-to-list ratio strengthened to 100.0%. Quarterly sales volume expanded sharply, with 263 closings versus 219 in the prior quarter, a +20.1% increase, and six-month activity running well ahead of the year-earlier pace. A positive six-month $/SF trend of +1.8% against a -4.0% twelve-month linear change indicates values have firmed from a softer period earlier in the year.
