Market Conditions Reports — Page 4

June 26, 2025

Fallbrook, 92028 — Market Conditions Update

The Fallbrook market presents balanced conditions with 4.2 months of inventory and a median sale price of $952,375, virtually unchanged from the prior quarter. Price per square foot rose 5.4% to $442, with a positive +5.0% twelve-month trend confirming genuine appreciation. The SP/LP ratio improved to 100%, and sales volume increased 17.2% to 143 transactions. Days on market rose from 31 to 50 due to increased listing activity (+22.2%), and the 60–90 day marketing time reflects an orderly market with neither buyers nor sellers holding a decisive advantage.

February 25, 2025

La Mesa, 91941 — Market Conditions Update

The La Mesa market is a tight seller's market with 2.3 months of inventory and a median sale price of $890,000, down 8.3% from the prior quarter. However, price per square foot remained stable at $585 (+0.3%), suggesting the headline price decline largely reflects a compositional shift toward smaller or more modestly priced homes. Days on market improved 38.7% from 31 to 19, and the SP/LP ratio held at 100%. Year-over-year sales volume rose 16.7%, and the 30–60 day marketing time confirms active demand with a 30–60 day anticipated absorption.

May 1, 2024

Rancho Mirage, 92270 — Market Conditions Update

The Rancho Mirage single-family market shows stabilizing conditions with a median sale price of $1,177,000, up 1.9% from the prior quarter, and 4.9 months of inventory placing it at the upper boundary of balanced. Price per square foot dipped 2.1% to $447, though the twelve-month linear trend of +14.3% reflects strong year-over-year appreciation from the mid-2023 trough. Days on market rose from 41 to 54, and the SP/LP ratio held at 97%. Sales volume surged 58.7% to 146 transactions, indicating broad buyer engagement across price segments in this desert resort community.
