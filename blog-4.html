Market Conditions Reports

June 26, 2025

Fallbrook, 92028 — Market Conditions Update

Fallbrook's residential market exhibited modest price appreciation with the median sale price of $952,375 essentially unchanged from the prior quarter (−0.3%). With 4.2 months of supply and 200 active listings, the market sits in balanced territory. The 12-month linear sale price trend is +3.45%, and price per square foot rose 5.4% to $442.49. Sales volume strengthened 17.2% to 143 transactions, and the SP/LP ratio improved to 100%. Days on market extended to 50 days from 31, reflecting the seasonal influx of new inventory. The combination of stable prices with increasing volume suggests sustained buyer demand.

February 25, 2025

La Mesa, 91941 — Market Conditions Update

La Mesa's market showed a seller-favoring environment with 2.3 months of inventory and 70 active listings. The median sale price of $890,000 declined 8.3% from the prior quarter, and the 12-month linear sale price trend registered −10.11%. However, price per square foot remained more stable at $585, essentially flat quarter-over-quarter. Days on market improved significantly from 31 to 19 days (−38.7%), and the SP/LP ratio held at 100%. Year-over-year sales volume increased 16.7%, suggesting a market undergoing repricing rather than demand contraction, as buyers remain active at lower price points.

May 1, 2024

Rancho Mirage, 92270 — Market Conditions Update

The Rancho Mirage single-family market showed a recovering trajectory with the median sale price of $1,177,000 up 1.9% from the prior quarter's $1,155,000. The 12-month linear regression trend indicated a strong +14.43% annualized appreciation rate. With 237 active listings and 4.9 months of supply, the market leaned toward balanced-to-buyer's conditions. Sales volume surged 58.7% to 146 transactions, and year-over-year totals rose 12.8%. Median days on market increased from 41 to 54 days, and the SP/LP ratio held at 97%. The combination of rising volume with modestly rising prices suggests broadening market activity.
