Market Conditions Reports

February 25, 2025

La Mesa, 91941 — Market Conditions Update

La Mesa's market is operating as a tight seller's market with 2.3 months of inventory and an anticipated marketing time of 30–60 days. The median sale price of $890,000 declined 8.3% from $971,000 in the prior quarter, though the 12-month linear sale price trend of −10.11% contrasts with a more modest −1.06% decline in $/SF, suggesting the price drop is largely driven by a shift in the mix of properties sold. Days on market improved from 31 to 19 days, and the SP/LP ratio held at 100%. Year-over-year sales volume is up 16.7%, confirming sustained market engagement despite the price correction.

May 1, 2024

Rancho Mirage, 92270 — Market Conditions Update

The Rancho Mirage single-family market is stabilizing with modest appreciation, recording a median sale price of $1,177,000, up 1.9% quarter-over-quarter and 3.8% on a six-month basis. Both the 12-month linear sale price trend (+14.43%) and $/SF trend (+14.30%) confirm broad-based recovery from the mid-2023 trough. With 4.9 months of inventory and 237 active listings, conditions are balanced to slightly buyer-favoring. Sales volume surged 58.7% to 146 transactions. Days on market increased from 41 to 54 days, reflecting increased supply, though the SP/LP ratio held at 97%.
