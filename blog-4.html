Market Conditions Reports

September 29, 2025

Oceanside — Market Conditions Update

Oceanside's residential market reflects a competitive seller's environment with just 2.3 months of inventory and a median sale price of $950,000, down a modest 1.9% from the prior quarter. The SP/LP ratio held at 100%, and days on market improved from 28 to 26. While the 12-month linear trend shows a −3.8% decline in sale prices, the $/SF trend was +3.8% at $534, suggesting stable per-unit values. Year-over-year sales volume is up 26.1%, confirming robust demand with an anticipated marketing time of 30–60 days.

June 26, 2025

Fallbrook — Market Conditions Update

Fallbrook's residential market exhibits balanced conditions with 4.2 months of inventory and a median sale price of $952,375, essentially unchanged from the prior quarter. Median price per square foot of $442 rose 5.4% quarter-over-quarter, and the 12-month linear trends are positive at +3.5% for prices and +5.0% for $/SF. Days on market rose from 31 to 50, reflecting seasonal inventory influx, while the SP/LP ratio edged up to 100%. Sales volume increased 17.2% to 143 transactions, with an anticipated marketing time of 60–90 days indicating sustained buyer interest.

February 25, 2025

La Mesa, 91941 — Market Conditions Update

La Mesa's market shows seller's conditions with 2.3 months of inventory and a median sale price of $890,000, down 8.3% from the prior quarter. The 12-month linear sale price trend of −10.1% indicates notable softening, though median $/SF of $585 held essentially flat at +0.3%, suggesting a shift toward smaller or lower-priced properties. Days on market improved from 31 to 19, and the SP/LP ratio held at 100%. Year-over-year sales volume rose 16.7%, indicating active buyer participation at adjusted price levels with a 30–60 day anticipated marketing time.

May 1, 2024

Rancho Mirage, 92270 — Market Conditions Update

Rancho Mirage's single-family market exhibits balanced-to-buyer's conditions with 4.9 months of inventory and a median sale price of $1,177,000, up 1.9% from the prior quarter. The 12-month linear trends are strongly positive at +14.4% for sale prices and +14.3% for $/SF at $447, reflecting the market's recovery from a late-2023 trough. Days on market rose from 41 to 54, and the SP/LP ratio held at 97%. Sales volume surged 58.7% to 146 transactions, indicating broadening activity across price segments with an anticipated marketing time of 25–103 days.
