Family Transaction Appraisal Service

When property changes hands between family members, a professional appraisal protects both parties and satisfies IRS requirements for gift tax documentation. Our appraisals establish fair market value to ensure the transaction is properly documented.

IRS Compliance

Fair market value documentation that meets IRS requirements for gift tax purposes and related filings.

Mutual Protection

A third-party valuation that protects both the transferor and the recipient in the transaction.

Standard Appraisals Recommended

Full standard appraisals are preferred over desktop for family transactions to better support IRS scrutiny.

Dispute Prevention

A professional appraisal prevents future family disputes by establishing an objective, documented value.
