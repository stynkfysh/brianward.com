August 6, 2025

La Jolla, 92037 — Market Conditions Update

The La Jolla 92037 market is a balanced market as of the August 6, 2025 effective date. The median sale price for the most recent quarter is $3,567,000, down from $3,738,500 in the prior quarter (-4.6%), while the median price per square foot rose to $1,380.54 from $1,339.20, a +3.1% quarterly move. Inventory stands at 4.8 months, consistent with a balanced market and an anticipated marketing time of 60-90 days. Median days on market lengthened to 52 from 29, indicating properties are taking longer to sell and some buyer caution, even as the sale-to-list ratio held firm at 99.0%. Quarterly sales volume reached 73 closings, and the size-normalized $/SF metric remains the more reliable read on underlying market direction.

August 6, 2025

Rancho Mirage, 92270 — Market Conditions Update

The Rancho Mirage 92270 single-family market is a stable, balanced market as of the August 6, 2025 effective date. Across the trailing 24-month period the overall median sale price for single-family residential properties was $1,140,000, with a median sale price per square foot of $444.67. On a quarterly basis both metrics remained relatively stable, fluctuating within a moderate range after firming in early 2025 and softening modestly through late 2025. As of the effective date, median sale price and $/SF were closely aligned with the period-wide medians, supporting a conclusion of no material directional pressure. Listing-side metrics such as days on market, list-to-sale ratio, and months of inventory were not available from the underlying data source for this market and are not reflected here. Overall conditions indicate a balanced market requiring no significant market-conditions time adjustment.
